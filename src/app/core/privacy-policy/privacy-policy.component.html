Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

MR Valuations ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website mrvaluations.co.uk (the "Service"). This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Create an account (email address, name)
  • List a number plate for sale (plate details, asking price, contact information)
  • Submit a valuation request (plate registration)
  • Contact a seller or request a feature

We also collect limited technical data automatically, such as browser type and pages visited, via Firebase Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Service
  • To send you relevant emails (e.g. listing nudges, enquiry notifications) — you can unsubscribe at any time
  • To process payments securely via Stripe
  • To improve the Service based on usage patterns

3. Sign-In with Third Parties

You may sign in using your Google or Facebook account. When you do, we receive your name and email address from those providers. We do not receive your password or payment details. Their use of your data is governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Facebook: facebook.com/policy.php

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore) and is hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure within the European Union.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you signed in and to maintain your session. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

6. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share data only with the third-party services that help us operate the platform:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, hosting
  • Stripe — payment processing

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict how we process your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (e.g. unsubscribe from emails)

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at: privacy@mrvaluations.co.uk
