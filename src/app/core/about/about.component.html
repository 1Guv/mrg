Our Story

Built out of frustration.
Grown through trust.

I'm the founder of MR Valuations, and I started this after facing the same frustration most plate owners know well — trying to find out what your plate is actually worth and getting nowhere fast.

Back in 2001, every valuation meant days of waiting, upfront fees, or estimates that varied wildly with no explanation. So I set out to fix that. The result was a tool that delivers an accurate, automated valuation in minutes — completely free. No forms. No waiting. No guesswork.

That tool became MR Valuations. Today we're the UK's first fully automated number plate valuation platform, with a live marketplace where buyers and sellers connect directly — no middlemen, no hidden costs. We're UK-based, independent, and always on your side.
